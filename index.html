JOHNY RHEY

Johny Rhey Is

A Nashville based country artist who was raised in Central Florida. Rhey found his country roots early in life with his mother being from Alabama. As a kid he was drawn to the piano and began to write songs.

He has recorded an EP and has singles up on ITunes. With the release of his first single "College Girl," Rhey started a lot of hype in his college town with his video that went to almost 20,000 views in a week. While continuing to grow his fan base across the country, when not on tour, he is in Nashville recording and songwriting. Most importantly Rhey wants to positively influence his fans through his music and his walk with Jesus Christ.Go check out John Rhey's music on ITunes, Pandora, Spotify, Amazon Mp3, Google Play, and YouTube!
